We're building RECN — a professional audio recording and transfer ecosystem designed around one principle: **recordings are sacred.**

## The Problem

Musicians capture ideas everywhere — in rehearsal rooms, on the bus, at 3am when a melody won't leave your head. But the tools available either compromise on quality, lock your recordings in a cloud you don't control, or make it painful to get audio from your phone into your DAW.

## What RECN Does Differently

**RECN Rec Mobile** is a professional-grade audio recorder for iOS and Android. Five recording profiles — from lossless RAW to compressed Voice Memo — let you match quality to context. Live waveform visualization, dB monitoring, and pause/resume give you studio-level control on a phone.

**On-device transcription** powered by Whisper AI means your recordings get searchable text without ever leaving your device. No internet, no cloud, no third party ever touches your audio.

**RECN Rec Desktop** (coming soon) will bridge the gap between phone and production — WiFi transfer directly into your DAW workflow.

**RecnCast** (coming soon) takes it further: stream audio from your DAW to listeners via peer-to-peer. A VST plugin captures your mix and sends it directly to browsers. No audio server, no latency penalty.

## What's Next

We're actively developing the mobile app with a focus on reliability and audio quality. Follow this blog for research deep-dives and feature updates as we build.
