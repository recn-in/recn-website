Making a recording is easy. Finding the useful part again is not.

A timestamp and waveform rarely carry enough context. Notes end up elsewhere, and layered versions become more files.

RECN keeps the recording, its notes, and the sessions made from it together. The current goal is simple: record something and find it again later.
